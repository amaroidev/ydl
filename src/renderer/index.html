How to Export YouTube Cookies

‚ö†Ô∏è Why do I need cookies?

YouTube now requires authentication to prevent bot access. Providing cookies allows RoiTube to download videos as if you were browsing YouTube in your browser.

üìã Steps to export cookies:

  1. Install a browser extension:
    • Chrome/Edge: Get cookies.txt LOCALLY
    • Firefox: cookies.txt
  2. Open an Incognito/Private browser window
  3. Go to youtube.com and sign in
  4. Navigate to youtube.com/robots.txt
  5. Click the extension icon and export cookies for youtube.com
  6. Save the file and select it in RoiTube settings
  7. Close the incognito window (important!)

‚ö†Ô∏è Important Notes:

  • Using cookies may risk your YouTube account being flagged
  • Consider using a secondary/throwaway account
  • Don't share your cookies file with anyone
